    Text Classification

    This code is designed to provide a concise guide on text classification using Naive Bayes and Logistic Regression. The dataset employed is titled "Economic news article tone and relevance" containing around 8000 news articles categorized as either relevant or irrelevant to the US economy. The objective is to delve into the steps of training and testing text classifiers for this specific dataset.

    Tokenization

    The provided analysis covers tokenization by word and by sentence, part-of-speech tagging using pos_tag(), and identification of bi-grams and tri-grams using ngrams() and FreqDist(). Tokenization by word offers finer granularity, while tokenization by sentence provides broader context. The pos_tag() function tags each word with its part-of-speech, aiding in linguistic analysis. Notably, some errors in part-of-speech tagging were observed, such as misclassification of words. Bi-grams and tri-grams reveal common phrases or concepts, offering insights into recurring word combinations within the text.
